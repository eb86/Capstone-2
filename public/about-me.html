Elaine Bernabe

Woman. Human. Artist.

Through my art, I aim to transport you to realms both familiar and extraordinary. Each artwork is a testament to my artistic vision, crafted with meticulous attention to detail and a deep reverence for the power of self-expression.

My art draws inspiration from a multitude of sources like the beauty of nature, the complexity of the human experience, and the boundless depths of the imagination. Through various mediums such as painting, drawing, and mixed media, I explore the interplay of colors, textures, and forms to evoke a range of sensations and provoke introspection. Each artwork offers a unique perspective waiting to be discovered.
